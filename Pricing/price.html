  • Re-registration charges will be charged after every 3 months at 1st visit rates.
  • Followup charges will be charged for every visit after 10 days from 1st day visit.(Exception Ayurved & Paediatric OPD)
  • For Dental OPD followup charges will be required to be paid after every 3 months.
  • Patients coming for Orthodontics treatment are not required to pay separate consultation fees.

OPD consultation charges after scheduled OPD Hours:

In case of emergency or for the convenience of the patient, consultant can see the patient in emergency at any time beyond his / her scheduled OPD timings for which he / she can charge upto Rs. 800/-

Private OPD:

Few of the consultants also run private OPD in the hospital where in consultant could be seen with prior appointment. There would be no fixed consultation fees. Doctor will have the discretion to charge anything upto Rs. 800/-
